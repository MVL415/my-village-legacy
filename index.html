Time / Change

Seasons changing, time for growth.
Shedding stagnant ways, like layers of clothes.
Less weight on the body, the sun nourishes the soul.

Not enough hours in the day.
I think they just stole another one.
Up before the sun, witness to darkness becoming light.

Grateful for the time.
Ready for some change.
